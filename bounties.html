Bounty Boards

Regional bounty boards provide structured goals inside Nosivad's sandbox, while one dependable daily contract on every board ensures there is always useful work available.

Contract Rules

  • A character may hold one Nosivad contract at a time.
  • Use /adventure to follow its objective, progress, source board, and route guidance anywhere.
  • Regional objectives only progress inside the region served by the board that issued the contract.
  • Most contracts are claimed at their source board. Mira's Eldeir introductions must be reported directly to Mira.
  • Gathering objectives count matching backpack items. Materials are consumed only after the completed turn-in successfully stages its rewards.
  • Completed objectives stop counting immediately, preventing duplicate progress and repeated completion messages.

Daily Contracts

Every board has at least one contract marked Daily. A claimed daily remains unavailable for the rest of that server calendar day and returns at server midnight. This is a calendar reset, not a rolling 24-hour timer: a contract claimed shortly before midnight becomes available again after midnight.

Eldeir Introduction

Mira's one-time path includes Rat Catching, Silk for Sutures, Wolf Trouble, Sewer Scout, Watching Eyes, Wing Samples, Broodlord Below, and First Low Rift. These teach combat, gathering, dungeon pacing, boss fights, and rifts without replacing the repeatable Eldeir daily.

Where Boards Appear

Boards are seeded in Eldeir, Helm, major New Celador danger areas, and dungeon entrance routes including Catacombs, Contempt, Corruption, Deception, Ruin, and Monolith.

Board Identity

Each board has its own contract pool. Eldeir focuses on onboarding, Helm on frontier work, Black Forest on hunting pressure, Monolith on relics and echoes, and dungeon boards on their local danger zones. Progress earned in an unrelated region does not satisfy a regional contract.

Long-Term Direction

Nosivad's city boards are meant to keep players moving through the world instead of gathering forever in one hub. Over time each city can gain more unique contracts while /adventure continues to act as Mira's shared remote tracker for every board you take work from.
