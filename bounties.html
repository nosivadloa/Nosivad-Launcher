Bounty Boards

Bounty boards point players toward the main shard loops and provide structured goals inside a sandbox world.

Rules

Players can hold one Nosivad bounty at a time. Progress is stored on the character, can be followed remotely with /adventure, and rewards are claimed at a board.

Where Boards Appear

Boards are seeded in Eldeir, Helm, major New Celador danger areas, and dungeon entrance routes including Catacombs, Contempt, Corruption, Deception, Ruin, and Monolith.

Board Identity

Each board now has its own contract pool. Eldeir focuses on starter onboarding, Helm on frontier contracts, Black Forest on hunt pressure, Monolith on relic and echo work, and dungeon boards on deeper danger-zone tasks. You still track everything through one shared /adventure thread.

Long-Term Direction

Nosivad's city boards are meant to keep players moving through the world instead of gathering forever in one hub. Over time each city can gain more unique contracts while /adventure continues to act as Mira's shared remote tracker for every board you take work from.
