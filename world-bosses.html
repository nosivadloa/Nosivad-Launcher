World Bosses

World bosses are rare events where a single powerful enemy breaks through a temporary rift into the world.

Rules

  • Only one world boss can be active at a time.
  • Bosses are randomly generated and are not tied to a region theme.
  • The rift remains open while the boss lives.
  • The boss leashes near its breach and should not spawn inside safe zones.
  • If unbeaten after five real-world days, the boss returns through the rift and disappears.

Scheduling

Why They Matter

World bosses are intended to feel like shard-wide events. Their loot and relic chances are much stronger than normal enemies, but they are rare enough that players should not expect them as routine farming content.
