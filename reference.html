Reference

Quick lookup for terms and launch-prep notes.

Player Commands

CommandUse
/wikiOpens the in-game Nosivad Wiki window.
/wiki riftsOpens directly to a topic. Supported shortcuts include rifts, boss, relics, shrine, bounty, levels, and grades.
/adventureOpens Mira's Wayfinder Thread, which tracks your active Nosivad contract and long-term custom quest guidance.

Common Terms

TermMeaning
Planar RiftA static world encounter where themed enemy waves enter the normal world.
Daily ContractA board contract claimable once per server calendar day; it returns at server midnight.
Regional ContractA contract whose objective only progresses in the region served by its source board.
Adventurer GradePublic E/D/C/B/A/S summary of player progression.
RelicRare named equipment connected to rifts and planar power.
MutationEnemy rarity/power variation from the dynamic enemy system.
World Boss RiftRare temporary rift that spawns one powerful randomized boss.
