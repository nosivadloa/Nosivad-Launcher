Adventurer Levels

Nosivad adds a shard progression layer on top of normal LoA skills and stats.

Visibility

You can see your own numeric Adventurer Level and XP progress. Other players see only your public Adventurer Grade.

GradeMinimum LevelMeaning
E1New adventurer.
D15Early dungeon-ready.
C30Established rift hunter.
B50Danger-zone veteran.
A75High-end rift contender.
S95Peak adventurer status.

XP Sources

Adventurer XP comes from Nosivad-controlled kill hooks: starter mobs, dynamic enemies, planar rift enemies and bosses, corrupted creatures, and world bosses. Bosses and harder content award more XP.

The current maximum Adventurer Level is 100.
