Getting Started

New characters begin their Nosivad journey around Eldeir Village, a lower-pressure area built to teach the shard's custom systems without overwhelming new players.

The First-Hour Loop

  1. Find Mira Vale, the Eldeir Pathfinder, near the bounty board.
  2. Claim the once-per-character welcome kit.
  3. Use /adventure if you want a portable checklist for the Eldeir intro path.
  4. Accept Mira's early hunt-and-gather contracts: rats, spider silk, wolves, sewer scouting, and bat-wing samples.
  5. Turn completed contracts in directly to Mira instead of relying on town errand quests.
  6. Scout the sewers, then challenge the Sewer Broodlord for your first real dungeon capstone.
  7. When ready, travel to the sewer rift for your first meaningful risk/reward jump.

Early Advice

Do not rush into high-grade gates or deep rifts. Learn healing, retreat paths, bounty turn-ins, sewer pacing, and how rift waves spawn before taking on harder encounters. Early gear now comes more reliably from Mira's contract rewards and the Sewer Broodlord instead of hoping a weak creature drops something lucky.
