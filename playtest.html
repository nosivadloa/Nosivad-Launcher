Playtest Guide

Nosivad is close to public testing. This page gives new testers one clear path: get into the world, understand the first twenty minutes, and tell us where the shard feels great, confusing, or broken.

Connection Info

Server
nosivad.bounceme.net
Base Game Required
Legends of Aria Eternal must already be installed.
Discord
discord.gg/TY5kQ62p9

First Session Goals

  1. Create a character and confirm you enter the world without getting stuck.
  2. Use /wiki, /adventure, and /level once each.
  3. Find Mira in Eldeir Village and claim the welcome kit.
  4. Visit the nearby shrine and try the shrine interaction.
  5. Accept a starter bounty and make visible progress on it.
  6. Fight a few local enemies and pay attention to difficulty, recovery, and loot feel.
  7. Report anything strange with /bug or send ideas with /suggest.

What We Need Feedback On Most

  • Character creation and login stability.
  • Whether Eldeir Village makes your next step obvious.
  • Whether /adventure helps or confuses you.
  • Whether starter rewards feel useful without being excessive.
  • Whether combat and rifts feel exciting without being unfair.
  • Any place where you simply did not know what to do next.

Useful Commands

CommandPurpose
/wikiOpen the built-in Nosivad knowledge pages.
/adventureOpen your portable progress and guidance tracker.
/levelView your private level and progression.
/bugSend a bug report directly to staff.
/suggestSend a feature or quality-of-life suggestion.

Good Report Examples

  • "Clicked Create and got stuck loading for 20 seconds before appearing in town."
  • "Accepted a rat bounty, killed three rats, and the tracker only counted one."
  • "The shrine buff worked, but I was not sure what blessing I received."
  • "I cleared early mobs and loot felt too sparse to understand the system."
The most helpful testers are not the ones who never hit problems. They are the ones who tell us exactly where the experience stopped making sense.
