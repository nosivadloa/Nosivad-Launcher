Breach Gates

Breach Gates are dungeon-style rifts. Instead of enemies entering the world, players enter a temporary pocket beyond the portal.

Grades

GradeDifficulty Intent
EStarter-adjacent solo or duo testing.
DEarly dungeon practice.
CMid-tier breach dungeon.
BDangerous group content.
AHigh-end group content.
SRare brutal endgame content.

Themes

Breach themes are random and independent of world location: Crypt of the Severed Veil, Primal Hunting Ground, Abyssal Warcamp, Astral Observatory, and Glass Wastes.

Rooms And Bosses

Each breach assigns enemies to named room bands inside the pocket, such as ossuaries, warrens, observatories, or glass-waste pits. Bosses also roll themed titles, so repeated gates should feel like variations on a dungeon run rather than the same room every time.

Clear Flow

  1. Use a grade-labeled Breach Gate.
  2. The gate chooses a random theme and opens a dungeon pocket.
  3. Players are teleported inside and can leave through an exit rift.
  4. Killing the boss marks the breach cleared.
  5. Five minutes later, the breach collapses and ejects remaining players.

Loot

Breach Gates are the main source of E through S graded dynamic gear. Normal mobs have low drop chances, mutated mobs have better odds, and breach bosses always drop graded gear. Completion rewards include coins, breach essence, and grade-scaled chances for a map scroll, bonus essence, and rare relic rewards.
